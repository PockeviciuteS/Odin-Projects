Transforming Furikake Rice

This dish is from an anime that we're watching. For those who don't know yet, its from "Food Wars: Shokugeki no Soma" (食戟のソーマ).

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken wings
  • 1 tbsp Sesame oil
  • 700g Bonito dashi
  • 50g Light soya sauce
  • 1 tsp Ginger, grated
  • 10g Sugar
  • 20g Sake
  • 15g Mirin
  • 4 Eggs
  • 10g Sugar
  • 3g Salt

Prep

    In a sauce pan, pan fry the chicken wings in sesame oil over medium-high heat, till both sides are browned. About 10-15mins. Put the sauce pan with the residual oil aside for later use. Place the chicken wings and the remaining ingredients in a sauce pot. Bring the broth to a boil over high heat, skim off any scum that formed. Cover and turn the heat down to low and simmer for an hour. Remove the chicken wings and strain the broth into a tray or a rectangular container. Allow to cool slightly then place in the refrigerator for 2-3 hours or until the aspic has set. Debone the chicken wings when they are cool enough to handle, minced up the meat and reserve. Beat together the ingredients till the sugar and salt has dissolved. Heat the sauce pan we used earlier on over medium-high heat. When the pan is hot, turn heat down to low and pour in the egg mixture, it should start setting immediately around the edges. Use a pair of chopsticks to quickly scramble the eggs and make small nuggets of them. iced the aspic into approximately 1cm cubes when it set. To serve, place minced chicken meat, egg soboro and diced aspic over a bowl of hot rice. Garnish with spring onions (if using). Enjoy!
